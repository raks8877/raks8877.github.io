What is Recursion?
Mirrors are the best way to explain what is recursion. If we place two mirrors on opposite ends of an object, the resultant reflection we see on the mirror is recursion.

Below you can see an example taken from the movie "Inception" by Christopher Nolan.
In terms of Computer Programing, we perform recursion for functions. A function consists of sets of instructions to accomplish a task. We cannot perform infinite recursion in computers due to limitations such as memory. Therefore we need some conditions to stop. We call such conditions “Base Condition”.

Let’s take a simple example to understand. Write a program to return the sum of numbers from 1 to n.
